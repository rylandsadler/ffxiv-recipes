Warmwine

Heated in the warm springs beneath O'Ghomoro, this mulled wine is said to cure countless ailments.

And those ailments it cannot cure, it will help one forget.

Ingredients:

  • 1 orange, sliced
  • 1/3 pound cherries, pitted
  • 6 whole cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 bottle dry red wine
  • 1 1/2 cups tart cherry juice
  • 2/3 cups honey

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, combine all of the ingredients. Bring to a low simmer, and simmer for 15 minutes. Serve hot.
